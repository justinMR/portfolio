Principles — Justin M. Ramirez

These are some of my general musings and thoughts about the web. Hopefully they give you some insight on what I'm about:

  1. The platform first. Reach for HTML and CSS before JavaScript, and JavaScript before a dependency.
  2. Restraint is a feature.
  3. Guiding AI is okay. You should still be driving (largely).
  4. I'm a child and disciple of the fun and alive 90's-2000's web era aesthetics. Let's bring it back!
  5. Keep it as simple as possible.
